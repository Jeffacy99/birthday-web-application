AKAN NAME GENERATOR

Akan Namesare derived from Ghanaian culture. In Ghana, children are given their first name as a "day-name" which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. This web application helps you,find out the day of the week you were born or someone was born and also helps determine to you your Akan Name, based on your gender and birthday month.
